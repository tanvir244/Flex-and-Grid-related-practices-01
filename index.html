Most popular city in Switzerland called Zurich

Zurich is the largest city in Switzerland. It is also the most populous city in Switzerland. Zurich is one of the 26 cantons in Switzerland. Cantons are the administrative divisions of Switzerland, that is, the cities in which various administrative activities are conducted in Switzerland. Of the 26 cantons, Zurich is the most important to tourists. Because this cantonment has many popular places of interest in Switzerland.
